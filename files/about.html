About Me

Software Engineering

Born and raised in California, spent two years in Southern Utah, and now located in beautiful New York. I am a software engineer with a knack for solving problems with efficient solutions.

I currently work mainly with JavaScript and TypeScript, along with React, Redux, Node, and SQL and NoSQL databases, but I enjoy diving into new tech (or tech that may just be new to me!). Recently I've been improving my knowledge of Java to enhance my skill set.

As a software engineer, communicating clearly and building community with my team is extremely important to me. When I am working, I aspire to encourage those around me and vice versa.
